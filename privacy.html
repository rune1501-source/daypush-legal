Privacy Policy

Daypush – Daily Planner  ·  Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Daypush. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Daypush app.

2. Information We Collect

Daypush stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store personal data on external servers. The following information is stored only on your device:

• Tasks, habits, and focus sessions you create
• App preferences and settings
• Your display name (if provided)

3. Authentication

Daypush offers Sign in with Apple and email-based authentication. When you sign in with Apple, we receive only the information you choose to share (name and/or email). Email-based accounts are stored locally on your device only.

4. In-App Purchases

Daypush offers optional Pro subscriptions and a one-time lifetime purchase processed through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. All purchases are handled by Apple in accordance with their privacy policy.

5. Analytics and Tracking

Daypush does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking technologies. We do not track you across other apps or websites.

6. Data Security

Your data is stored exclusively on your device and protected by your device's built-in security features. We do not have access to your personal data.

7. Children's Privacy

Daypush is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
